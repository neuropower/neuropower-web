Welcome

NeuroPower is a toolbox that allows you to compute the necessary sample size for your fMRI study to obtain a certain predefined level of statistical power. Our method can be used for whole-brain analyses, as well as for region-of-interest analyses. What's even better: there's no need to download or install anything. Your sample size calculations are now only a few clicks away.
